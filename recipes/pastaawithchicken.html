Pasta with Chicken

A basic spaghetti recipe made with fresh tomatoes and chicken. This is quick to make for a meal on a busy day as it doesn't take much time.

Ingredients:

  • 160 grams of Pasta
  • 200 grams of Chicken
  • Olive Oil (as needed)
  • 2-3 Garlic cloves
  • 1 small onion
  • 350 grams of Tomatoes
  • 3/4 tps of pepper
  • Salt (as needed)

How to make:

  1. Prepare the pasta (Boiled water + Salt and Oil on a pan. Cook for 8-10 min)
  2. Salt chicken as you please (Salt and Pepper is the basic)
  3. Add sauted chicken on the pan with garlic and onion
  4. Add pasta for 2 min max just so it can heat up.
